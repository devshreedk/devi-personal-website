Dilemma - It's Twisted!

It is an anthology and I worked as a compiler with one of my friends. The dilemma means a situation in which we have to choose difficult choices between two or more alternatives and most of them are undesirable ones. In this anthology, 30+ co-authors penned their views and thoughts in the form of poems, articles, quotes, stories, etc.

Through this anthology, we tried to encourage people to express or share their feelings and experience and wants to know how they dealt with them.
